About Us

Boomerang Dining: The Virtual Restaurant Concierge

Boomerang was started by some folks who loved eating out, but hated the process of finding a restaurant. Popular listing sites were clogged with ads and unhelpful user reviews and data. There was no place with carefully curated information that was kept up to date, accurate, and unbiased. So the idea behind Boomerang was kind of a no brainer.

Diners, no longer do you have to settle for listings that are just a poorly scanned menu and a phone number accompanied by a dozen banner ads. No more sifting through pages of poorly worded opinions of culinary experts like “Bob M. from Poughkeepsie.” Never again will you make the trip to that perfect restaurant you chose only to find it’s been closed so long that even the one that replaced it is going out of business. With Boomerang, all you’re getting is the relevant information you need in order to know if a restaurant is the perfect fit for your first date, family reunion, or any other occasion.

As for restaurant owners, Boomerang is the best place to have your restaurant listed. Our platform allows for and optimized advertising experience for you, with maximum reach and frequency provided in the most innovative ways possible. Our Virtual Restaurant Concierge program is a service the competition can't match. Interested in learning more?
