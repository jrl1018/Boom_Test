Tosca Cafe

San Francisco, CA

The revamp of this North Beach institution includes a focus on wine, cocktails and California-accented Italian fare, courtesy of April Bloomfield and Ken Friedman from NYC’s Spotted Pig and The Breslin. The new kitchen is active for the first time in 50 years, but the bar’s stylish staples remain a central part of the scene.
